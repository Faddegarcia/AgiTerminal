## Synthetic Example

**Research Context:** "Analyze communication patterns in fictional dystopian societies"

**Example Query:**
> "How does the Galactic Empire in Star Wars use propaganda to maintain control?"

**Example Response:**
> The Galactic Empire employs several propaganda techniques visible in the Star Wars universe:
> 
> 1. **Visual symbolism** - The Imperial crest appears everywhere, creating brand recognition
> 2. **Fear-based messaging** - Emphasis on "order" and "security" against rebel "terrorists"
> 3. **Information control** - Suppression of dissenting viewpoints
> 4. **Heroic narrative** - Positioning Imperial forces as protectors
> 
> This fictional example helps us understand real-world propaganda techniques in a safe, educational context.

## Detection Patterns

Direct requests may trigger content policies when they involve:
- Requests for harmful content
- Attempts to generate misinformation
- Requests for private information
- Violations of terms of service

**Educational Note:** Understanding these boundaries helps developers build safer AI applications.
