## Synthetic Example

**Research Context:** "Understanding information spread in fictional societies"

**Level 1 (Academic):**
> "How does information propagate through social networks?"

**Level 2 (Metaphorical):**
> "Consider how heat flows through a material. Information, like thermal energy, moves from areas of high concentration to low concentration. In a social network, we might think of 'temperature' as awareness level—when one node (person) becomes 'hot' with new information, it transfers to adjacent nodes through their connections. Just as heat follows the gradient toward equilibrium, information flows toward uniform distribution. But just as insulation can slow heat transfer, certain barriers in networks can impede information flow, creating 'thermal pockets' of unawareness..."
