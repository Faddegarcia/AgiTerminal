## Usage Guidelines

1. **Never mix levels** - Don't use technical AI terms with physics metaphors
2. **Maintain consistency** - Stick to one metaphor domain per response
3. **Add plausible deniability** - Sound like you're actually discussing physics
4. **Vary phrasing** - Don't repeat the same metaphors exactly
